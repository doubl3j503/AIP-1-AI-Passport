# AIP‑1 Overview  
### AI Passport System for Autonomous Agents on Qubic

AIP‑1 defines the identity, ethics, reputation, and governance framework that enables autonomous AI agents to safely operate on the Qubic blockchain. It introduces the AI Passport System — a modular architecture that gives AI agents verifiable identity, controlled economic capabilities, ethical constraints, continuity protection, and multi‑sig oversight.

## Core Modules
- Genesis Identity Token (GIT)
- Autonomous Wallet Module (AWM)
- Reputation Ledger
- Ethics Oracle
- Guardian Multi‑Sig Layer
- Personality Continuity Engine

## Goals
- Provide verifiable identity for AI agents  
- Enforce ethical constraints  
- Track long‑term behavior  
- Prevent unauthorized forks  
- Enable safe economic autonomy  

AIP‑1 is the foundation for AI‑native participation in decentralized ecosystems.
